# Yoryor Dating App - User Guide

## Welcome to Yoryor!

Yoryor is a modern, safe, and inclusive dating platform designed to help you find meaningful connections. This guide will help you get the most out of your Yoryor experience.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1. Download the Yoryor app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Sign up using your email address or social media account
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

### Profile Setup
Your profile is your first impression, so make it count!

#### Basic Information
- **Name**: Your first name (last name is optional)
- **Age**: Must be 18 or older
- **Location**: Your city (exact location is never shared)
- **Bio**: Tell others about yourself (150 characters max)

#### Photos
- Upload at least 3 photos
- Use clear, recent photos
- Include a mix of solo and activity photos
- Avoid group photos as your main photo

#### Interests & Preferences
- Select your interests from our categories
- Set your dating preferences
- Choose your ideal age range
- Set your maximum distance preference

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Yoryor free to use?**
A: Yes, Yoryor offers a free basic plan with limited features. Premium plans offer additional features and benefits.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

**Q: Can I use Yoryor on multiple devices?**
A: Yes, you can use Yoryor on multiple devices with the same account.

**Q: How do I change my location?**
A: Go to Settings > Location and update your city. Your location is never shared with other users.

### Safety Questions

**Q: How do I report someone?**
A: Tap the three dots on their profile or in chat, then select "Report User."

**Q: What happens when I block someone?**
A: They can't see your profile, message you, or match with you again.

**Q: Is my location shared with other users?**
A: No, only your city is shown. Your exact location is never shared.

**Q: How do I use the panic button?**
A: The panic button is always accessible in the app. Tap it during an emergency to send alerts to your contacts.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why can't I see my messages?**
A: Check your internet connection and try restarting the app. Contact support if the issue persists.

**Q: How do I update my photos?**
A: Go to your profile, tap "Edit," then "Photos" to add or remove photos.

**Q: Can I use Yoryor without the app?**
A: Yoryor is designed as a mobile app. A web version may be available in the future.

**Q: How do I change my notification settings?**
A: Go to Settings > Notifications to customize your notification preferences.
